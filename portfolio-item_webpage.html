Emperor's Might

A WH40k weaponstore landing page

The webpage "Emperor's Might" is an online platform that immerses visitors into the fictional universe inspired by the Warhammer 40,000 (40k) universe. The webpage presents an array of wargear and weaponry that echoes the grandeur and might of the Imperium. The page opens with a commanding header featuring the iconic Imperial Aquila logo, setting the tone for an epic journey into the world of heroic battles and cosmic conflicts.

As visitors delve deeper, they encounter sections dedicated to various facets of the Imperium's arsenal. The "Wargear" section showcases a range of formidable weapons such as Bolters, Lasguns, Chain Swords, Plasma Guns, and Meltaguns, each accompanied by vivid imagery and their respective prices, enticing enthusiasts to equip themselves with the tools of war. Accompanying the visuals are informative "Data Slates" that delve into the lore and significance of these weapons, enriching the narrative and deepening the connection to the universe.

Working on the "Emperor's Might" webpage was a big deal for me, as it was my first real shot at a major web design project. Getting into the exciting world of Warhammer 40,000, I quickly realized I had a lot to learn. Making things look cool while still being easy to use turned out to be trickier than I thought, but it was a good challenge that helped me understand how to make stuff that people actually want to use.

I ran into some problems along the way, like figuring out how to get videos and forms to work smoothly and making sure everything looked good on phones and computers. But every problem was like a lesson that helped me figure out how to make things better.
